What is paid2go?


paid2go is a delivery service that caters directly to your budget, household, and
personal needs. Using statistics from grocery store demands, demographics
from households across the world, and a state of the art artificial intelligence
algorithm (or DAIS, for short), paid2go is able to create the perfect grocery list
for your family. No more searching through your cabinets to remember what
you have or coming home from the store only to realize you've gotten the
wrong items; paid2go will have your list in check!

Along with curating your shopping list for the week, paid2go offers a pickup
service, so that you can get your order whichever way you please. Have a store
you prefer over the rest? Always on the hunt for the best prices? However you
shop, DAIS will convert your shopping list to an order ready for your household.
You have the option to get your groceries delivered to you via a paid2go self-
driven car, or you can place the orders for pickup and follow the route to your
nearest paid2go storefront to collect your shopping for the week.
